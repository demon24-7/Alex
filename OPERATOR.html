Photoshop


Adobe Photoshop is industry-leading raster graphics editing software developed by Adobe Inc. It's widely used for creating, enhancing, and editing images and designs. Photoshop offers a vast array of tools and features, making it a powerful application for professional photographers, designers, and artists

Premiere pro


Adobe Premiere Pro is a professional video editing software application that is part of the Adobe Creative Cloud suite. It's a widely used tool for editing video, commercials, and other film, television, and online video content. Premiere Pro offers a comprehensive set of features for editing, audio manipulation, and adding visual effects and titles.

HERE SOME APP THAT HELP YOU TO EDIT IN MOBILE


CAPCUT


CapCut is a free, all-in-one video editing app and online platform developed by ByteDance, the company behind TikTok. It offers a wide range of features for creating and editing videos, including basic editing tools, advanced features like keyframe animation and slow-motion, and a library of pre-made templates. CapCut is known for its user-friendly interface and is popular for both personal and business use.

KINEMASTER


KineMaster is a mobile video editing application that provides a wide range of tools and features for creating and editing videos on smartphones and tablets. It's designed for both beginners and professionals, offering features like layering, keyframe animation, chroma key (green screen), speed control, and more. KineMaster also provides access to a library of royalty-free music, sound effects, and other assets to enhance videos.
